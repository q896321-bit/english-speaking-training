# Self-Talk Prompts (30+)

每天抽 1 題，10 分鐘不停講。重點不是講對，是**不停**。

---

## 入門級（Stage 1）— 個人/日常

1. Describe what you did today, from morning to now.
2. Describe your favorite meal. Why do you like it?
3. Describe your room. What's on your desk?
4. Talk about your morning routine.
5. What did you do last weekend?
6. Describe your hometown. What's it like?
7. Talk about a person you admire. Why?
8. Describe the weather today and how it affects your mood.
9. What's your plan for tomorrow?
10. Describe your phone — what apps do you use most?

## 中階（Stage 2）— 觀點/敘事

11. What's a book or movie that changed how you think? Why?
12. If you could change one thing about your university, what would it be?
13. Talk about a time you failed. What did you learn?
14. What's the most stressful part of being a master's student?
15. Should AI be used to write academic papers? Why or why not?
16. Describe your research in 3 minutes — for a non-expert.
17. What's the hardest concept in your field? Try to explain it simply.
18. If you could have dinner with any researcher (alive or dead), who and why?
19. Talk about your relationship with your advisor.
20. What would you do if your CiI paper got rejected?

## 學術專屬（Stage 2-3）— CiI / Defense 準備

21. Walk through your G3a method as if presenting at a conference.
22. Explain your XAI ablation results. Why does hard union beat soft?
23. Defend your choice of 5 seeds (not 3, not 10).
24. Justify why you chose CiI over EAAI as primary target.
25. Explain WaferMix in 60 seconds. Then in 3 minutes. Then in 10 minutes.

## 商務情境（Stage 3）

26. You're in a job interview. Why should they hire you?
27. Pitch your research as a startup idea in 2 minutes.
28. Your team missed a deadline. Explain to your boss what happened.
29. You disagree with a colleague's approach. How do you push back?
30. Describe your ideal job in 5 years.

## 進階主題輪播（任何階段）

31. The role of XAI in industrial AI deployment.
32. Why semiconductor manufacturing matters for Taiwan.
33. Your view on the ethics of AI-generated content.
34. The future of academic publishing — open access vs subscription.
35. The career path: PhD vs industry, debate yourself.

---

## 使用方式

### 選題策略
- Stage 1: 從 1-10 隨機抽
- Stage 2: 從 11-25 抽，每週至少 2 次學術題
- Stage 3: 26-35 為主，配合面試與 defense 準備

### 進階方法

**錄音法（每週 ≥1 次）**:
1. 隨機抽 1 題
2. 計時 5 分鐘錄音（手機就可以）
3. 隔天重聽，找 3 個改進點
4. 同題下週再講一次，比較進步

**Chunks 注入法**:
- 講之前選 5 個本週新學的 chunks
- 強制在 10 分鐘內用上至少 3 個

**配對法（Stage 2 後）**:
- 跟 iTalki tutor / 語伴用同一題互講
- 你講完換對方講，學新表達

---

## 致命錯誤

- ✗ 中途停下查字典 → 破壞 flow，回到中翻英模式
- ✗ 講完就忘 → 卡住的點要記下來查
- ✗ 同一題重複講「太多遍」 → 大腦記成 script，失去構句訓練意義（同題隔 ≥1 週再講）
