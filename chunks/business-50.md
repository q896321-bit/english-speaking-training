# Business English Chunks (50)

Stage 3 商務溝通用。涵蓋會議、Email、簡報、議價、求職。

---

## Meetings (10)

1. **Let's get started.** — 開始開會
2. Can we **circle back to** that? — 我們可以回頭談那個嗎？
3. Let's **take that offline**. — 我們會後再談
4. Just to **align expectations**... — 對齊一下期望
5. Can you **walk me through** it? — 帶我看過一遍
6. I want to make sure we're **on the same page**. — 確認我們認知一致
7. Let me **push back on** that. — 我想 push back
8. Can we **double-click on** that? — 深入挖一下那個點
9. Let's **table this for now**. — 暫時擱置
10. **What's the next step?** — 下一步是什麼？

## Email Openers / Closers (10)

11. I hope this email **finds you well**. — 開頭客套
12. **Following up on**... — 後續追蹤
13. **As discussed earlier**... — 如先前討論
14. I'm **reaching out because**... — 我聯繫你是因為
15. Just a **quick note to**... — 簡短說
16. Let me know if you have any questions. — 結尾邀請問
17. **Looking forward to** your reply. — 等你回覆
18. **Happy to discuss further**. — 樂意進一步討論
19. Best regards, — 標準結尾
20. Please **don't hesitate to reach out**. — 別客氣聯繫

## Polite Disagreement (5)

21. I **see where you're coming from**, but... — 理解你立場，但
22. I have some **concerns about**... — 我有些顧慮
23. Have we **considered**...? — 我們有沒有考慮過
24. I'd like to **flag a potential issue**. — 提出一個潛在問題
25. I'm not sure I **follow**. — 我沒跟上（委婉的「不懂」）

## Requests (5)

26. **Would it be possible to**...? — 是否可能
27. Could I **ask for**...? — 可以請求
28. Do you have **bandwidth for**...? — 有時間/容量做嗎？
29. When would be a good time to...? — 什麼時候方便
30. I'd **appreciate it if** you could... — 感激若你能

## Negotiation (5)

31. **What would it take to**...? — 要怎樣才能（達成）
32. Is there any **flexibility on**...? — 有沒有彈性
33. Let's find a **middle ground**. — 找折衷
34. I'd like to **propose**... — 我想提議
35. **Help me understand**... — 幫我理解（委婉質疑）

## Status Updates (5)

36. We're **on track**. — 進度正常
37. We're slightly **behind on**... — 稍微落後
38. The **blocker is**... — 卡點是
39. I need your **input on**... — 需要你意見
40. Here's **where we stand**. — 目前狀態

## Presentations (5)

41. Today I'd like to **share**... — 今天要分享
42. The **key insight** is... — 關鍵洞察
43. Let's **dive into** the numbers. — 看數字
44. As you can see **from this chart**... — 從圖表可見
45. The **takeaway** is... — 重點

## Decision-Making (5)

46. Let's **make a call on** this. — 做決定
47. The **pros and cons** are... — 優劣
48. **What's the risk**? — 風險是什麼
49. What's our **ROI on** this? — 投報率
50. Let's **pilot** this first. — 先試行

## 使用 × 情境對應

| 情境 | 必用 chunks |
|---|---|
| 站週報 | 36-40 (status), 5 |
| 跟主管 1-on-1 | 38, 39, 25, 21 |
| 客戶會議 | 4, 6, 11, 14 |
| 內部 brainstorm | 23, 31, 33, 46 |
| 求職面試 | 41-42, 17, 47-49 |
